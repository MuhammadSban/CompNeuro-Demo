Approach 2 — Fragment Shader

DataTexture · GLSL IDW · viridis polynomial · per-fragment
lowactivityhigh
How it works Electrode positions and values are packed into an RGBA32F DataTexture (1 texel per electrode). A ShaderMaterial samples the texture in the fragment shader, computes IDW per-pixel, and maps the scalar through a viridis polynomial — no CPU loops, resolution-independent gradients. Update values by writing into texture.image.data and setting needsUpdate = true.
